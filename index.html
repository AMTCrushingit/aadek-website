What We Deliver

Engineering Services

From concept to commissioning — AADEK provides the full spectrum of mechanical engineering services for projects of every scale.

🌡️
HVAC Design

Complete heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system design — from load calculations and equipment selection to duct layout, controls, and energy modelling for optimal performance.

💧
Plumbing Engineering

Domestic water supply, sanitary drainage, stormwater, and specialty plumbing systems designed to code — with full tender documentation and construction oversight.

🔥
Fire Protection

Sprinkler systems, fire suppression, standpipe, and detection systems designed to NFPA standards — protecting lives and assets with engineered precision.

⚗️
LPG Systems

Liquefied petroleum gas system design for commercial and residential applications — including storage, distribution, safety systems, and regulatory compliance documentation.

✅
Commissioning

Systematic verification that all mechanical systems perform to design intent and owner expectations — identifying deficiencies before handover and establishing performance baselines.

🏛️
Owner's Representative

Your dedicated advocate throughout the project — overseeing design coordination, contractor performance, budgeting, scheduling, and quality control so your interests are always protected.

📋
Project Administration

Structured project management from design through commissioning — documentation control, contractor coordination, budget tracking, and progress reporting with clear communication at every stage.

⚖️
Testing, Adjusting & Balancing

Independent TAB services verifying airflow, hydronic balance, and system performance — with detailed reports establishing baseline data for maintenance and energy benchmarking.

💰
Cost Estimation

Accurate mechanical cost estimates and tender evaluations — giving owners and developers the financial clarity needed to make confident project decisions from concept through procurement.
