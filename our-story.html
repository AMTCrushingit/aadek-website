The Evolution

How Balanced as Designed Found Its Voice

The Beginning
TAB as a Division, Not a Brand

Testing, Adjusting and Balancing was always part of what AADEK Consulting delivered. Karen and her team performed TAB work on commercial, hospitality, and institutional projects across the Bahamas, but it lived under the AADEK umbrella with no separate identity, no separate positioning, and no separate digital presence.

The Recognition
TAB Clients Are Different Clients

Over time, a pattern emerged. TAB and commissioning clients, including contractors, developers, and building owners seeking independent verification, were coming to AADEK for a specific, specialist service. They were not always looking for full mechanical engineering consultancy. They needed a specialist. AADEK was that specialist, but nothing in the brand said so.

The Decision
A Division Becomes a Brand

The decision was made to give the TAB and commissioning work its own identity. Not a separate company, but a specialist brand within the AADEK group, with its own name, its own visual language, and its own digital presence. A brand that could speak directly to the clients who needed independent verification, without the broader engineering consultancy framing getting in the way.

The Build
Balanced as Designed Goes Live

BluWav Growth built the Balanced as Designed site in 48 hours. Dark charcoal background, vibrant green primary colour, warm orange accent, all drawn directly from the existing logo. Two primary services, clearly separated and equally prominent: Testing, Adjusting and Balancing, and HVAC Commissioning. A specialist brand that looks and feels like the specialist it is.
