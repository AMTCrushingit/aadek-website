Transformation 02

Three Divisions.
Three Revenue Streams. One Platform.

The Challenge

AADEK operates three distinct businesses — AADEK Consulting, Balanced as Designed, and Adrian's Safe Air & Mechanical. The old site buried all three under a single confusing navigation with no clear differentiation.

Potential clients couldn't tell what AADEK actually did — let alone which division to engage for their specific need.

✗ No division separation or individual positioning
✗ Services mixed together with no clear hierarchy
✗ Three revenue streams invisible to potential clients

The Solution

Each division was given its own identity, colour accent, service list, and value proposition — presented as a unified group with three specialist arms.

Transformation 03

Making the Engineer
the Authority

The Challenge

Karen Myers-Rolle holds dual Professional Engineer licenses — one of the most significant credentials in the engineering profession. The old site made no mention of her name, her qualifications, or her experience.

In a trust-driven industry like engineering consultancy, the principal's credentials are the single most powerful conversion tool. They were completely absent.

The Solution

Karen was positioned as the centrepiece of the site — her dual PE licensure, Caribbean expertise, and 20+ years of experience featured prominently in the hero, about section, and footer.

Credibility Architecture Built
  • Dual PE badge in hero card and footer

  • 4 credential badges: PE, Mechanical Specialist, Caribbean Experience, Founder
  • Stats: 20+ years, 3 divisions, 100+ projects, 2x PE
  • "Karen Myers-Rolle, PE" in page title and meta tags
